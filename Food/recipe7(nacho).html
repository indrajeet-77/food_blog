Recipe 7

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • ptional toppings: diced avocado, sour cream, salsa, guacamole, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Step 2:
    Arrange a layer of tortilla chips on a baking sheet or oven-safe dish.
  • Step 3:
    Sprinkle a layer of shredded cheese evenly over the chips, covering them as much as possible.
  • Step 4:
    Scatter the black beans, diced tomatoes, sliced jalapenos, black olives, and diced red onion over the cheese.
  • Step 5:
    Add another layer of tortilla chips on top of the toppings, followed by another layer of cheese and the remaining toppings.
  • Step 6:
    Place the nachos in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Step 7:
    Remove the nachos from the oven and let them cool slightly. Optional: Garnish with diced avocado, sour cream, salsa, guacamole, or chopped cilantro.
  • Step 8:
    Serve the nachos warm as a snack or appetizer. They can be enjoyed on their own or with additional dips or sauces.
  • Step 9:
    Feel free to customize the toppings based on your preference, adding cooked ground beef, shredded chicken, or other vegetables. Nachos are a versatile and crowd-pleasing dish that's perfect for gatherings or game nights. Enjoy!
