Recipe 17

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 1 lemon, halved

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Make sure the oven rack is positioned in the middle.
  • Step 2:
    Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. This helps ensure a crispy skin.
  • Step 3:
    In a small bowl, combine the salt, black pepper, paprika, dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, and minced garlic.
  • Step 4:
    Rub the chicken with olive oil, coating it evenly.
  • Step 5:
    Sprinkle the spice mixture all over the chicken, making sure to rub it into the skin.
  • Step 6:
    Stuff the cavity of the chicken with the lemon halves. This adds a wonderful citrus aroma and flavor.
  • Step 7:
    Tie the legs together using kitchen twine, if desired. This helps the chicken cook evenly.
  • Step 8:
    Place the chicken on a roasting pan or a rimmed baking sheet, breast-side up.
  • Step 9:
    Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) when measured with a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh without touching the bone.
  • Step 10:
    If the chicken starts to brown too quickly, you can tent it loosely with foil to prevent excessive browning.
  • Step 11:
    Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute and makes the chicken more tender.
  • Step 12:
    Optional: Garnish with fresh herbs, such as rosemary and thyme, for added aroma and presentation.
  • Step 13:
    Carve the chicken and serve with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad.
