Recipe 12

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or diced
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or couscous
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds or sunflower seeds
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large salad bowl, combine the cooked chicken breast, quinoa or couscous, mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, red onion, crumbled feta cheese (if using), and sliced almonds or sunflower seeds.
  • Step 2:
    In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, extra-virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • Step 3:
    Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  • Step 4:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 5:
    Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  • Step 6:
    Before serving, give the protein salad a final toss.
  • Step 7:
    Serve the protein salad chilled as a satisfying and nutritious meal.
  • Step 8:
    Feel free to customize the protein salad by adding other vegetables, herbs, or proteins of your choice. You can also experiment with different dressings or add avocado slices for added creaminess. Enjoy this protein-packed salad that is both flavorful and filling!
