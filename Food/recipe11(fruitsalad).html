Recipe 11

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries, hulled and quartered
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 cup cubed watermelon
  • 1 cup cubed pineapple
  • 1 cup cubed cantaloupe
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup sliced kiwi
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • Juice of 1 lime or lemon
  • 2 tablespoons honey (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large bowl, combine the strawberries, grapes, watermelon, pineapple, cantaloupe, blueberries, kiwi, and banana.
  • Step 2:
    Squeeze the juice of one lime or lemon over the fruit to help prevent browning and add a refreshing flavor.
  • Step 3:
    If desired, drizzle honey over the fruit for added sweetness. Toss gently to coat the fruit evenly.
  • Step 4:
    Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the fruit to chill.
  • Step 5:
    Before serving, give the fruit salad a gentle toss.
  • Step 6:
    garnish with mint leaves for an extra burst of freshness and visual appeal.
  • Step 7:
    Serve the fruit salad chilled as a refreshing and healthy snack or as a light dessert.
  • Step 8:
    Feel free to customize the fruit salad by adding other seasonal fruits such as mango, peaches, or raspberries. You can also sprinkle some shredded coconut or chopped nuts on top for added texture. Enjoy this colorful and delicious Fruit Salad!
