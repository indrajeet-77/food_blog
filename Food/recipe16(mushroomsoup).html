Recipe 16

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced (any variety you prefer)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  • Step 2:
    Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they release their moisture and become tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Step 3:
    Reserve a handful of cooked mushrooms for garnish, if desired. Transfer the remaining cooked mushrooms to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Be cautious when blending hot liquids.
  • Step 4:
    Return the blended mushrooms to the pot and pour in the vegetable or chicken broth. Add the dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  • Step 5:
    Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  • Step 6:
    Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 7:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 8:
    Ladle the hot mushroom soup into bowls. Garnish with the reserved cooked mushrooms and fresh chopped parsley.
  • Step 9:
    Serve the soup hot and savor the rich and earthy flavors of this comforting mushroom soup.
