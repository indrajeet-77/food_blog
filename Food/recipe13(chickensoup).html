Recipe 13

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  • Step 2:
    Pour in the chicken broth and add the cooked chicken, bay leaf, dried thyme, and dried parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the soup to a boil.
  • Step 3:
    Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the flavors have melded together.
  • Step 4:
    In a separate pot, cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Step 5:
    Once the soup has simmered, remove the bay leaf. Add the cooked egg noodles to the pot and stir to combine.
  • Step 6:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 7:
    Ladle the hot chicken noodle soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley.
  • Step 8:
    Serve the soup hot and enjoy the comforting and flavorful chicken noodle soup.
  • Step 9:
    Feel free to customize the soup by adding additional vegetables like peas or corn, or herbs of your choice. This recipe is perfect for warming up on a chilly day or when you're in need of some nourishing comfort.
