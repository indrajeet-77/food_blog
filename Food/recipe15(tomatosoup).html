Recipe 15

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 ounces total) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  • Step 2:
    Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 15 minutes.
  • Step 3:
    Use an immersion blender or transfer the mixture to a blender, and blend until smooth. Be careful when blending hot liquids.
  • Step 4:
    Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream, sugar, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  • Step 5:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 6:
    Ladle the hot tomato bisque soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh chopped basil leaves.
  • Step 7:
    Ladle the hot tomato bisque soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh chopped basil leaves.
  • Step 8:
    Serve the soup hot and enjoy the rich and creamy tomato bisque.
