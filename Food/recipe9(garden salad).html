Recipe 9

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (such as lettuce, spinach, or arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, grated carrot, sliced red onion, black olives (if using), and crumbled feta cheese (if using). Toss gently to mix the ingredients.
  • Step 2:
    In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Step 3:
    Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat the vegetables evenly
  • Step 4:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 5:
    Serve the garden salad as a side dish or add protein such as grilled chicken or shrimp to make it a complete meal.
  • Step 6:
    Feel free to customize the salad by adding other vegetables like bell peppers, radishes, or avocado. You can also add toppings like toasted nuts or seeds for added crunch and flavor. Enjoy this fresh and vibrant Garden Salad as a nutritious and refreshing addition to your meals!
