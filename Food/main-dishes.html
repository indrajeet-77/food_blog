Main Dishes

Roasted Chicken

Roasted chicken is a classic and timeless dish known for its tender and juicy meat, complemented by a flavorful and crispy skin. It is a beloved main course that brings comfort and satisfaction to the table. The chicken is typically seasoned with a blend of herbs, spices, and sometimes citrus, which infuses the meat with aromatic flavors during the roasting process. As it cooks in the oven, the chicken's skin turns golden brown and develops a satisfying crunch, while the inside remains moist and succulent..



Baked Salmon

Baked salmon is a mouthwatering and nutritious dish that showcases the natural flavors of fresh salmon while providing a tender and flaky texture. It is a popular choice for seafood lovers and a versatile option that can be enjoyed in various ways. To prepare baked salmon, a fillet or steak of fresh salmon is seasoned with a combination of herbs, spices, and a touch of lemon juice or olive oil to enhance its natural taste. Common seasonings include dill, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. The salmon is then placed in the oven and baked until it is cooked to perfection.



Vegetable Curry

Vegetable curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish that showcases a vibrant mix of vegetables cooked in a rich and spiced sauce. It is a popular choice among vegetarians and vegans, as it offers a delicious and nutritious option for a plant-based meal. A variety of spices, such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, are added to create a complex and well-balanced flavor profile.



Stir-Fried Noodles

Stir-fried noodles are a popular and delicious dish that originates from various Asian cuisines. This versatile and satisfying meal features a combination of noodles, vegetables, and protein, all cooked together in a hot wok or skillet. The dish typically starts with a selection of fresh vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, cabbage, and bean sprouts. These vegetables are thinly sliced or julienned to ensure quick and even cooking. Other ingredients like mushrooms, onions, and snow peas can also be added to enhance the flavor and texture of the dish.
