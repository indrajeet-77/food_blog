Recipe 14

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 1 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 cup chopped green beans
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and minced garlic. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  • Step 2:
    Pour in the vegetable broth and add the diced tomatoes, diced potatoes, chopped cabbage, chopped green beans, diced zucchini, dried thyme, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the soup to a boil.
  • Step 3:
    Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and flavors have melded together.
  • Step 4:
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Step 5:
    Ladle the hot vegetable broth soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley.
  • Step 6:
    Serve the soup hot and enjoy the comforting and nourishing vegetable broth soup.
  • Step 7:
    Feel free to customize the soup by adding additional vegetables of your choice, such as bell peppers, corn, or peas. You can also add herbs like basil or rosemary for extra flavor. This recipe is perfect for a light and healthy meal, and it's a great way to enjoy the natural flavors of various vegetables.
