Recipe 22

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and whole milk until well combined.
  • Step 2:
    Add the granulated sugar to the mixture and whisk until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  • Step 3:
    Stir in the vanilla extract and mix well.
  • Step 4:
    Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.
  • Step 5:
    Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to an airtight container.
  • Step 6:
    Place the container in the freezer for at least 4 hours or until the ice cream is firm.
  • Step 7:
    When ready to serve, scoop the homemade vanilla ice cream into bowls or cones.
