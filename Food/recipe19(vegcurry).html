Recipe 19

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or naan bread (for serving)

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.
  • Step 2:
    Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Step 3:
    Add the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder to the pan. Stir well to coat the onions, garlic, and ginger with the spices. Cook for 1 minute to toast the spices.
  • Step 4:
    Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir to combine everything together.
  • Step 5:
    Add the diced carrots, bell pepper, zucchini, cauliflower florets, and green beans to the pan. Stir to coat the vegetables with the curry sauce.
  • Step 6:
    Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pan and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  • Step 7:
    Taste the curry and season with salt and pepper according to your preference.
  • Step 8:
    Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, remove the pan from heat. Serve the vegetable curry hot over cooked rice or with naan bread.
  • Step 9:
    Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro before serving.
